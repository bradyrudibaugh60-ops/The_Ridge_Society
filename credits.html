Development Timeline

The Ridge Society began as an idea to create a psychological horror experience focused on choices, loyalty, and discovery.

Development includes custom gameplay systems, story progression, interactive environments, and mystery-driven exploration.

Design Philosophy

The Ridge Society combines classic RPG presentation with psychological horror elements.

Inspired by games that focus on atmosphere, storytelling, and player choice.

Every decision shapes the player's journey.
